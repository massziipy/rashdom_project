About Us


GMI (Green Masters International )was founded in Singapore and spread in to Malaysia and India. We are committed to full fill your dreams into reality, whether it’s your family house, a luxurious hideaway, or a high rise tower. We cater to all aspects of design and construction of all type buildings. We are dedicated to give you the best low cost good looking Eco friendly house within the time limit. For us age of a building is not an issue!!We renovate all types of buildings in to best that you dream. We believe that great design should enhance the value of each place, make optimal utility of space, elicit an emotional response, and use less energy. Our designs are inspired by clients and the surrounding landscape, resulting in an architecture that is uniquely informed by both subject and place. Our Approach towards Sustainable development has been practical ,holistic ,intuitive.Most of our design which blend traditional concepts with contemporary lifestyles using technology as an aide For us , being “GREEN”is a care.care for people ,nature and the earth.for these ideas to translate to reality ,one needs to approach them with a lot of passion ,common sense and economy .
